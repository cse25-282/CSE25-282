Shoova is Botswana's go-to professional sneaker cleaning service. We combine care, skill, and premium products to restore your shoes to their original glory — fast, affordable, and reliable.

✦ Who We Are

More Than a Cleaning Service

Shoova was founded by Tefho — a student who understood the struggle of wanting clean kicks without the time or money to deal with it. What started as washing a few pairs for friends grew into something much bigger.

Today we serve students, professionals, sneakerheads, and everyday people across Gaborone who care about how they present themselves. We believe that your shoes say a lot about you — and we make sure they say the right things.

Every pair that comes through our hands is treated with the same care we'd give our own. No shortcuts. No damage. Just results.
